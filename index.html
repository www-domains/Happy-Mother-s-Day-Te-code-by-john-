Dearest Aunt,

Today, I wanted to take a moment to really sit down and tell you how much you truly mean to me. You are more than just an aunt to me; you are a guiding light, a mentor, and the person I admire most in this world. My life is so much better because you are in it.

Thank you, Te, for accepting me wholeheartedly. I know that maskin usahay naa koy apan, you never turned your back on me. Thank you for your understanding, for always being there to listen, and for the endless support, advices, and reminders you give me. I may not always be showy or say "thank you" every single day through words, but I hope you feel my love through my actions. I try my best to show my service and loyalty to you every time naa kay ipasugo, because I want to honor everything you do for me.

I have learned so many important things from you. You taught me how to be humble and showed me the importance of staying away from bad habits. Because of you, I chose not to enter addiction like drinking alcohol and to focus on my study instead. You are the reason I stay on the right path.

Salamat kaayu sa mga support sa akong pag-skwela, Te. Even when things feel negative or when the pressure of college gets too heavy, your strength gives me the courage to keep going. I'll admit that I feel pressured sometimes because of my age and the feeling that I haven't reached my goals yet, but I always hold onto your words. I always take your advice na dili pa ang ulahi sa tanan. You make me believe that I have my own timing.

I really admire your achievements, Te. You are the best example of success and grace. Pasensya usahay, Te, na usahay dili ko masabatan. I am still learning and growing, but please know that always nako ginatuman imong gusto because I know deep in my heart that it is para ra sad sa akong kaayuhan.

I am working hard every single day to reach the best version sa akong kaugalingon. I want to make you proud and show you that all your sacrifices were worth it.

Salamat kaayu, Te, sa tanan-tanan and for everything that is yet to come. Please always take care of yourself always. I pray for your good health and your happiness every single day.

With all my love, admiration, and loyalty,
